About Billion Hopes

Billion Hopes is a philanthropic initiative focused on mobilising everyday donors, supporting changemakers, and building large-scale, sustainable impact across health, education, climate and livelihoods. We blend community giving, technology, and transparent impact reporting to amplify contributions from individuals and organisations.

Our Vision & Mission

Transforming philanthropy through collective action

Our Vision

To activate a global culture of giving where every Indian becomes a co-architect of a more equitable world. India as a philanthropic superpower funding solutions across borders.

Our Mission

To channel collective giving from the many to empower changemakers with flexible capital for systemic transformation.

Mandate

Changemaker & Non-Profit Incubation and Capacity-Building Program
Billion Hopes Philanthropic Fund

Rationale

While India and the Global South are rich in intent, ideas, and grassroots leadership, the social sector continues to suffer from chronic underinvestment in core operations, leadership, systems, and strategy.

The Billion Hopes Philanthropic Fund recognizes that strong institutions create lasting impact. To catalyze systemic change at scale, the Fund mandates focused investment in incubating changemakers and strengthening nonprofit organizations through flexible operational grants and leadership support.

Program Objective

To incubate, stabilize, and scale high-potential changemakers and nonprofit organizations by providing unrestricted operational funding and fractional CXO-level expertise—enabling them to grow into resilient, mission-driven institutions capable of long-term impact.

Scope of the Mandate

1. Operational Grants for Non-Profit Resilience

  • Non-restrictive, multi-year operational grants
  • Core team salaries and leadership
  • Governance, compliance, audits
  • MEL, technology, and digital infrastructure
  • Institutional development and strategy

Overhead is impact infrastructure.

2. Fractional CXO Support

  • CEO / Strategy / CFO / COO / CTO / Impact / Fundraising
  • 6–18 month, outcome-oriented engagements
  • Capability transfer, not dependency

3. Open Calls & Inclusive Access

  • Transparent selection & independent panels
  • Regional and thematic diversity
  • Focus on women-led and first-generation changemakers

4. Incubation, Not Just Funding

  • Peer learning cohorts
  • Mentorship and governance clinics
  • Storytelling and donor readiness
  • Connections to future funders

Intended Outcomes

  • Stronger, resilient nonprofit institutions
  • Reduced founder burnout
  • Higher impact per rupee
  • New norms of trust-based philanthropy
  • India-rooted solutions for global challenges
